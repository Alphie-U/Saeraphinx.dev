[PROJECTS]

LumaBot
LumaBot is a private bot made as a replacement for MEE6 that currently exclusively handles levels, birthdays, and role reactions. It was developed by me after MEE6 began locking many of its previously free features behind their premium tier and began primarily focusing on Non Fungible Tokens and Cpytocurrency. A server that I am in was paying for their premium tier and I didn't want to finacially support them anymore, so I made my own. The bot features customizable XP gain and levels. The bot is written in Node/discord.js.

BoopBot
BoopBot is a bot that allows for users to boop other users and gain XP for it. It is a private bot in use in only a handful of my friend's discord servers and features a cross-server leaderboard and XP boosts daily. The bot is written in Node/discord.js.

WholesomeCountingBot
WholesomeCountingBot is a bot that facilitates countng in Alphie's discord server. It was written and started after the server was at about 12700, and deletes any message that doesn't include the next number. The bot is written in Node/discord.js.
